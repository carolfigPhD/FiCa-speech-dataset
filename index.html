FiCa speech dataset


The FiCa speech dataset is a private dataset consisting of 92 minutes of audio from a single speaker. This dataset was originally created in order to train a TTS system capable of synthesizing short feedback responses such as "mhm", "oh", "wow". This work was published at SigDial 2024: SigDial paper

FiCa speech dataset
